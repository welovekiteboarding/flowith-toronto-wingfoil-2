What to Expect in Your Advanced Lesson

Our Advanced Wingfoil Lesson is designed for riders who are already comfortably foiling and want to take their skills to the next level. This 2-hour session provides personalized coaching to help you master complex maneuvers such as foiling tacks and jibes, sustained toeside riding, switch stance, and potentially an introduction to basic jumps or wave riding if Toronto's conditions permit. We focus on refining your technique, improving efficiency, and expanding your repertoire of skills.

In Toronto's dynamic Lake Ontario environment, advanced skills allow you to handle a wider range of conditions, from light winds requiring advanced pumping techniques to choppier waters where precise foil control is key. Our instructors will help you adapt and excel, possibly using smaller, more agile gear suited for advanced riding.

Lesson Structure (2 hours)

Goal Setting & Current Skill Assessment (20 minutes)

  • Discussion of your specific goals (e.g., landing foiling jibes, learning to jump).
  • On-water assessment of your current foiling proficiency and technique.
  • Personalized plan for the session focusing on 1-2 key advanced maneuvers.
  • Advanced safety considerations for new maneuvers.

Technique Deep Dive & Drills (50 minutes)

  • Detailed breakdown and demonstration of target maneuvers (e.g., footwork for tacks/jibes, body positioning for jumps).
  • Specific drills to isolate components of the maneuver (e.g., carving entries, wing transitions, board pop).
  • Practice attempts with real-time feedback and video analysis (if applicable/available).
  • Focus on refined foil control, wing power management, and body mechanics.

Advanced Maneuver Practice & Refinement (40 minutes)

  • Repeated practice of the target advanced maneuvers.
  • Troubleshooting common mistakes and personalized corrections.
  • Introduction to linking maneuvers or adapting them to different conditions.
  • Exploring concepts like advanced pumping for light wind, riding toeside with confidence, or switch stance control.
  • If conditions and skill allow, introduction to basic wave riding techniques or small controlled jumps.

Debrief & Future Progression (10 minutes)

  • Review of progress made during the session.
  • Key takeaways and areas for continued practice.
  • Recommendations for self-practice, further coaching, or specific conditions to seek out.
  • Discussion of next steps in your advanced wingfoiling journey.

Prerequisites

To get the most out of the Advanced Lesson, you should be a confident intermediate wingfoiler, comfortable with:

  • Sustained foiling in both directions (port and starboard tack).
  • Consistent upwind and downwind riding on foil.
  • Controlled carving turns off the foil, and attempting basic on-foil turns.
  • Good understanding of safety protocols and right-of-way.
  • Completion of our Intermediate Lesson or equivalent proven experience.

What's Included

  • Professional one-on-one or small group coaching with an experienced advanced instructor.
  • Use of appropriate advanced wingfoil equipment (smaller boards/foils if desired/suitable, subject to availability). You may also use your own gear.
  • Wetsuit, PFD/Impact Vest, and Helmet if needed.
  • Personalized feedback and a clear path for continued progression.

Unlock Your Potential

The Advanced Lesson is your gateway to a more dynamic and expressive wingfoiling experience. Mastering transitions, jumps, and other advanced techniques opens up a whole new world of possibilities on the water, allowing you to make the most of varied conditions and truly express your style.

Our instructors are passionate about helping you achieve your goals and will provide the expert guidance needed to break through plateaus and confidently add new skills to your repertoire.
