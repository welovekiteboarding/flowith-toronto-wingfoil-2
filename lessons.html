Your Path to Wingfoiling Mastery

At TorontoWingfoil, we offer a comprehensive lesson program designed to take you from complete beginner to confident independent wingfoiler. Our lessons are structured to build skills progressively, ensuring you learn safely and effectively. All lessons include equipment (wing, board, foil, wetsuit, helmet, PFD). Our experienced instructors tailor each session to your abilities and Toronto's current conditions.

We cater to all levels:

  • First-Timers: Our Discovery Lesson is the perfect starting point.
  • Beginners: Progress to foiling with our Beginner Lesson.
  • Intermediates: Refine your skills and learn transitions in our Intermediate Lesson.
  • Advanced Riders: Push your limits with specialized coaching in our Advanced Lesson, covering techniques like advanced tacks, jibes, and potentially jumps or wave riding basics if conditions allow.

Why Choose TorontoWingfoil for Your Lessons?

Our team consists of passionate and experienced wingfoilers who are certified instructors. They provide patient, clear, and personalized coaching to help you progress quickly and safely.

Safety First Approach

Your safety is our top priority. All lessons include comprehensive safety briefings, use of safety gear (helmet, PFD), and instruction in carefully chosen, safe locations in Toronto. More on our Safety Page.
