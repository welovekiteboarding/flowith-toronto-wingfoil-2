Our Pricing Structure

At TorontoWingfoil, we believe in transparent pricing with no hidden fees. All of our lessons include all necessary equipment (wing, board, foil, wetsuit, life jacket, helmet) and professional instruction.

Our pricing is structured based on your skill level and lesson type. Most students progress through our structured program, starting with a Discovery Lesson and advancing through Beginner and Intermediate stages.

All-Inclusive Pricing

The prices listed below include all necessary equipment, professional instruction, and safety gear. There are no additional rental fees or hidden costs.
