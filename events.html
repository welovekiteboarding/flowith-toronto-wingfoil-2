Toronto Wingfoiling Events & Clinics

Join us for exciting events, demo days, and skill-enhancing clinics throughout the season.

Get Involved in the Toronto Wingfoil Scene

At TorontoWingfoil, we're passionate about building a vibrant local wingfoiling community. Beyond our regular lessons, we aim to host and promote a variety of events and clinics designed to bring foilers together, help you improve your skills, try out new gear, and simply have fun on the water.

Our events cater to all levels, from those new to the sport looking to connect, to advanced riders wanting to push their boundaries. Keep an eye on this page and subscribe to our newsletter for the latest updates on what's happening!

Types of Events We Host & Support

Here's a taste of what you can expect from TorontoWingfoil events and our involvement in the local scene:

Gear Demo Days

Curious about the latest wings, boards, or foils? We plan to partner with leading brands to host demo days where you can try out new equipment under expert supervision. A great way to find your next favorite piece of gear!

Fun Races & Slalom Sessions

For those with a bit of a competitive spirit! We'll organize informal, fun races and slalom courses suitable for various skill levels. It's all about friendly competition and improving your maneuvering skills.

Social Gatherings & BBQs

Wingfoiling is as much about the community as it is about the sport. We'll host casual beach BBQs, post-session get-togethers, and other social events to help you connect with fellow foilers off the water.

Skill-Specific Clinics

Looking to master a specific technique? We'll offer specialized clinics focusing on areas like foiling tacks and jibes, jumping, wave riding (where conditions permit), or light wind techniques. These will often be led by our senior instructors or guest coaches. Connect this with our Advanced Lessons.

Past Event Highlights (Examples)

To give you an idea, here are some fictional examples of events we aspire to host:

"Spring Wing Fling" - Season Kick-off

Date: Early May (Past Event Example)

A fun-filled day to kick off the wingfoiling season in Toronto! Included gear demos from top brands, free mini-clinics on wing handling, a friendly "first to foil" challenge, and a community BBQ at Cherry Beach. Great turnout and perfect winds!

"Sunset Foil & Social" - Mid-Summer Meetup

Date: Late July (Past Event Example)

An evening session at Woodbine Beach enjoying the summer sunset on the foil, followed by a casual social gathering. All levels welcome, focused on enjoying the conditions and camaraderie. We shared tips, stories, and planned future adventures.
